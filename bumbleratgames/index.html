Gameplay Guide

Gameplay

Build Your Factory:

Start with an empty plot and turn it into a lively hub of aerospace activity. Construct mega-bays, launch mounts, and tank farms. Set up efficient production lines and streamline your workflow to craft advanced spacecraft.

Test and Launch:

It's more than just factory efficiency—it's about creating history. Put your creations to the test at the launch site, tracking with telemetry and cameras as they fly. Tweak your factory design based on real-time feedback and aim for a flawless launch.

Survive the Final Frontier:

Space is unforgiving. Will your Starships withstand the journey? It's up to you to build vessels strong enough to explore space and begin the journey to Mars.

Key Features:

  • Versatile building system enabling countless factory layouts and configurations
  • An extensive inventory of resources, propellants, and vehicle components for assembly and management
  • Nine fully functional vehicles, each with the ability to perform spaceflight testing.
  • Ten distinct test scenarios, from static fires to 200-meter hops and 10-kilometer belly-flop landings
